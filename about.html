A little bit about me.

I'm a nerd in a lot of ways.

I spend my free time playing Dungeons and Dragons, other board games, carrying teams in Dota 2, or hunting monsters in Hunt: Showdown, my latest obsession (and timesink, yeesh). I also spend WAY too much time reading Warhammer lore off of the wiki, and I have watched the Star Wars and Lord of the Rings movies an unhealthy number of times.

When I'm not gaming, I love to travel. I studied Ancient Greek and Roman history in my undergrad at UCLA, and I've been to both Italy and Greece. I love exploring new cultures, trying new foods, seeing the marvels of other societies, and seeing how people live around the world.

When I'm not doing any of that stuff, I'm hanging out with my girl, going to the beach, going to music festivals so I can mosh, planning out my next tattoo, or watching the Office for the 50000th time.

And finally, when I'm not wasting my time I like to run through courses on sites like freeCodeCamp, or expand my skills in other ways such as trying new software or looking ahead at what I want to be learning next.
